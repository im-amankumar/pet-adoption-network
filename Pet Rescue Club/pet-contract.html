Terms and Conditions

2. Identification and License

The Adopter agrees that the pet shall wear a collar with an identification tag at all times. The Adopter shall maintain a proper municipal license under the local law and comply with all statutes and ordinances that apply to the pet.

3. Veterinary Care

The Adopter shall provide the pet with the necessary vaccination as advised by the pet's veterinarian. When the pet becomes sick or injured, immediate medical care shall be provided, and the Adopter shall take full responsibility for the veterinary expenses.

4. In-house Treatment

The Adopter agrees to provide the pet with humane treatment at all times and shall provide the pet with proper food, freshwater, shelter, and exercise, and shall allow the pet a reasonable amount of time to adjust to the surrounding.

5. Return Policy

If the Adopter wishes to return the pet, the Adopter shall provide prior written notice to the Adoption Center. After the approval from the Adoption Center, the Adopter shall make necessary arrangements for a return. Until the pet is returned to the Adoption Center, the Adopter is responsible for providing proper care.

6.Neuter/Spay

In the event the pet has not been sterilized prior to the contract, then the Adopter agrees to Spay/ Neuter the pet within months of adoption.

7. Release of Liability

The Adopter agrees never to file a claim or suit against the [Adoption Center Name]. The Adopter releases [Adoption Center Name] and its founders, employees, agents, representatives, volunteers, and assigns from any liability arising from the pet's adoption.

8. Indemnity

The Adopter agrees to indemnify and hold the [Adoption Center Name] harmless for any injury, illness, amputation, scarring, death, damages, or other losses to the pet, arising out as a result of the Adopter's negligence or liability.
