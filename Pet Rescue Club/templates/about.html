About Us

The Pet Rescue Club was founded when a group of dedicated animal lovers came together with vision of simplifying the adoption process and connecting sheltered animals with caring home.

Founded in 2024.The Pet Rescue Club is the India's largest network of fully vaccinated pets listed by out trusted shelter partners.

Together, let's create a world where every pet has a lovinf and forever home.

Inspiration

We draw inspiration from the tireless efforts of animal welfare organizations, volunteers, and compassionate individuals who work towards ensuring the well-being of animals in need.


We are honored to collaborate with these organizations, bringing their adoptable pets into the spotlight, increasing their chances of finding their forever homes.


We believe in the power of adoption to create a lifelong bond of love and companionship.
