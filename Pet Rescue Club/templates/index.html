OUR CORE VISION


Our core vision is to connect people with their perfect pets, promote responsible pet ownership, and eliminate animal homelessness. It provides a user-friendly platform for adopting animals, educates about the benefits of adoption and proper care, and advocates for animal welfare. The website aims to make the adoption process transparent and enjoyable while fostering a supportive community. It celebrates successful adoption stories to inspire others and create a world where every animal finds a loving home.

BEFORE YOU ADOPT


Caring for a companion animal goes far beyond providing food, water and shelter. It takes planning to bring the right pet into your home, and to make sure your lifestyle is the right one for your pet.
Ensure you have the time, resources, and love to provide a forever home. Remember, adopting a pet is a lifelong commitment that brings immeasurable joy and responsibility.

WHY YOU SHOULD CHOOSE ADOPTION


1. PET IMPROVE YOUR MOOD 2. PET MAKE YOU FEEL MORE SECURE
According to studies, spending time with your pet can trigger an increased level of oxytocin, also known as the “love hormone” Large dog breeds like German shepherds and Rottweilers make for great deterrents from outside threats, allowing their owners to feel safer.
3. PET IMPROVES YOUR SOCIAL LIFE 4. PET IMPROVES PHYSICAL ACTIVITY
Pet owners were significantly more likely to get to know people in their neighborhood than those who did not own a pet. Taking your dog for a walk also makes you more physically fit and increases the chance you're going to get your daily exercise
5. PET MAKES YOU A BETTER PERSON 6. PET MAKES YOU KIND
According to a study by BarkBox, owning a dog can make you a better person (or at least make you think you are!). Of those surveyed for the study, 93% of pet owners said they make them noticeably more patient or affectionate Children growing up with a pet do so with more empathy towards animals and more empathy in general.
